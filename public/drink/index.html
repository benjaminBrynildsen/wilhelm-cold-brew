✦  OUR LITTLE LEDGER  ✦

Wilhelm,
1897.

Glove maker. Norwegian. Brooklyn-bound.

He left Bergen with a trunk of leather, the address of a cousin, and the quiet conviction that hard work and patience could turn one life into a different one. He crossed the Atlantic on little more than a leap of faith.

That's what aging in oak does, too. Coffee enters the cask as one thing, sits with the wood and the ghost of bourbon for ninety nights, and comes out as something the beans could never have imagined for themselves.

A leap. In a bottle.
